Brand Race

May the odds be ever in your favour

The weapons used to win the marketing war.

  • Number of participants: 2 per team, 1 per school
  • Time limit: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Eligibility: Class 11 and 12

Participating teams will have to bring models of their product for promotion at their respective stalls.

Teams must not display the name of their school.

The following information hast to be provided at their respective stalls:

  • Name of the company
  • Logo of the company
  • Product they are selling
  • Production cost
  • MRP of the product

Judgement criteria:

  • Creativity
  • Promotional Strategies
  • Conduct with costumers
  • Innovation
  • Feasibility
  • Futurism

Quiz

Brain it on

The ultimate test of commerce knowledge.

  • Number of participants: 2 per team, 1 per school
  • Time limit: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Eligibility: Class 11 and 12

The quiz will consist of two stages:

Preliminary Stage: The participants will have to answer a questionnaire which will comprise of both objective and subjective questions. Questions will be related to important events in the Indian and global context related to economics, finance, business and accountancy. The teams will be given 20 minutes to answer the questionnaire. The top 6 teams, scoring the highest marks will then qualify for the final round.

Final Stage: The top 6 teams will compete at the final round which will consist 4 rounds

  • Around the world
  • Companies and logos
  • Benchmark events in the world of economics
  • Rapid fire

Brochure Making

When it's too hot to think

Design a brochure for your new company.

  • Number of participants: 2 per team, 1 per school
  • Time limit: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Eligibility: Class 11 and 12

Details:

  • The participant will be required to prepare a brochure of a given type of company(like tours and travel company, real estate company etc.).
  • Participant will be required to carry his own art material, for example: colours, paints, fevicol, scissor etc. One A2 size sheet and a packet of origami sheets will be provided in the school itself. Participant will be free to use the sheets in any way- cut or fold it.

Judgement criteria:

  • Design
  • Written content
  • Creativity and attractiveness
  • Relevance to the topic

Stock Wars

Take your shot and beat the markets

Stock wars puts you in the fast paced world of traders and big money.

  • Number of participants: 2 per team, 1 per school
  • Time limit: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Eligibility: Class 11 and 12

Details:

  • Create and manage your portfolio
  • Test practical stock trading strategies
  • Analyse companies and place your orders
  • Monitor other trader's investment strategies
  • Watch how your stocks would perform in the real world
