Chai Pe Charcha

“The aim of argument or of discussion, should not be victory but progress”

Participants: 2/team

  1. First round will be a qualifying round. Participants need to take part in a group discussion.
  2. Winners of the first round will qualify for the final round.
  3. Topic for the final round will be given on the spot.

  • Legitimacy of the points/arguments proposed
  • Debating skills
  • Diplomatic behavior

Ven-You

“Where instinct fails, intellect must venture”

Participants: 2/team

Time: 5minutes

  1. After online registration, each school will be assigned a state of India.
  2. Participants are expected to come up with a startup idea related to the culture of that state.
  3. 5 minutes will be given for powerpoint presentation (with follow up questions).
  4. Example- Rajasthani food truck with live folk music.

Logo Story

“Your logo is an extension of your brand, and your brand is a story”

Participants: 2/team

Time: 3minutes

  1. The teams will be given an industry after online registration.
  2. The participants are expected to bring a graphically designed logo(in '.png' or '.jpg' format, with backup on google drive) to the host school.
  3. The participants are also required to present their story along with a tagline for their self designed brand on the final day of the event.
  4. Example- The Adidas three stripes story.

  • Innovation
  • Originality of idea
  • Background research
