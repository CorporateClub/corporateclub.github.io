What is Empório?


Empório is an endeavour to make the students aware of the practical aspects of business, commerce, and industry and to provide them with an enriching educational experience.The students will be able to explore and learn through the interactive, innovative and creative competitions. The competitions are planned to give the students a comprehensive exposure to the working environment, to enhance their thinking abilities, to stretch their imaginations, and to make them future winners.
